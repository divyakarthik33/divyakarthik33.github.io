About Me

Since I was a little girl, I have always had a passion for curiousity and creativity. Whether it be being the leader of constructing IKEA furniture or the creater of several make your own game - games, I have always wanted a career path to allow me to innovate on a daily. This led to my interest in exploring engineering, and even within this field, computer science naturally appealed to me due to it's versatility in several industries. Doing work that yields transformative impact and results is what's rewarding for me, which makes the company I work for and their mission is extremely important to me.

With programming and solution design as the backbone, I am interested in full stack product development (especially by using and learning different technologies), data visualization and analytics, and user experience innovation. I'm interested in roles that allow me to design and implement software solutions that drive a business forward and by direct transitivity impact lives.

Based off of my interests, some of my other hobby based roles include being the Director of Marketing at WiCS (Women in Computer Science) at UC Davis, a Peer Advisor at the Engineering Undergraduate Office and a tutor at ALOHA Math Minds. During me time, I like to binge Netflix, cook and write reviews.
